About Us

Our Story

Since 1992, Downtown Donuts has been a family owned shop serving fresh donuts and locally roasted coffee to the downtown community.

We pride ourselves on creating a cozy environment where people can come together, relax, and enjoy quality treats made with care.

Whether you're grabbing a quick breakfast or meeting friends, our goal is to make every visit feel welcoming and memorable.
